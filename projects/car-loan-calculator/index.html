FAQ

  1. How to use Car Loan EMI calculator
    1. Enter your principal amount, interest rate and tenure.
    2. Click and know your EMI payable.
  2. How much time does it takes to use the calculator?
    If you have the details with you (total amount, interest levied and tenure), it takes only few seconds.
  3. What is the typical tenure of a car loan in India.
    In India most car loans run for 1 to 5 years, although certain new plans to run for up to 7 years. The lower the tenure, the higher the EMIs you need to pay.
  4. Can my car loan application be rejected?
    On occasion, your car loan application may be rejected if you do not fulfill the minimum eligibity criteria or if there is a mistake in your documentation. Besides, your CIBIL score has to be 750 or more at all times.
  5. How is car loan EMI calculated monthly?
    EMI or Equated Monthly Installments refer to the monthly payments you make to the lender to repay your loan. These payments include the pricipal amount as well as the interest i.e.
    EMI = Prncipal Amount + Interest on Pricipal amount

    Mathematics , EMI can be calculate using the following formula:
    { P x R x (1+R)^ N / [(1+R)^ N-1] }
    where,
    P = Principal amount of the loan,
    R = Rate of intreest and
    N = Number of monthly installments.
